## Root-cause hypothesis

- The engine's terminal `medianFinalState` is derived from a single
  best-median Monte Carlo sim (`mc.medianFinalState`), while the
  headline NW comes from the per-month P50 percentile of the fan.
  These two quantities are NOT algebraically guaranteed to agree:
  the fan's P50 at month H is the 50th percentile of the cross-sim
  NW distribution, while medianFinalState is the portfolio composition
  of the sim closest to the median terminal NW.

- Sprint 29 surfaces this drift instead of hiding it. The reconciliation
  gate blocks the affected NW figures and the audit panel exposes the
  component-by-component breakdown.
